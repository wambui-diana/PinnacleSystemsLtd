First Aid Kits

Category: Road Safety Equipment

They are small boxes containing items such as bandages, plasters, and antiseptic wipes for use in giving help to a sick or injured person until full medical treatment is available.

The first aid kit has a wide range of applications. It can be used in the chemical industry, medical, scientific and chemical laboratories, management and administration, metal and metallurgical industries.

Standard kits often come in durable plastic boxes, fabric pouches or in wall mounted cabinets. The type of container will vary depending on the purpose, and they range in size from wallet-sized through to a large box. Kits should be checked regularly and restocked if any items are damaged or are out of date.
