Biometric Systems

Category: Security Systems

They rely on specific data about unique biological traits in order to work effectively. A biometric system will involve running data through algorithms for a particular result, usually related to a positive identification of a user or other individual.

Our Time-Attendance Solution is specially designed for organizations to help manage attendance, shifts and leave policies. Its human-like intelligence calculates attendance and leaves and integrates seamlessly with payroll systems.

Our Automated Time & Attendance Systems are convenient to use and prevent organizations from overpaying. It reduces your administrative burden and eliminates lost wages due to timesheet errors or 'Buddy Punching', allowing you to generate real-time reports of employee attendance and job costing.
